Business Labs For Career Growth


Business labs in City College has been setup to elevate the teaching methods to nurture the next generation business professionals. The labs support the academic curriculum through simulation exercises and management games. It creates a virtual workbook, provides students with practical understanding of business models and management practices. The lab also provides access to enormous amount of data, news and information to advance the students business practices.


Language Labs


The language laboratory plays an important role in the language learning process. It is technological aid for learning and has a number of innovative facilities such as digital multimedia control, wireless headsets and microphones, the interactive response pad, etc to enrich the student’s language learning experience. The lab is design to assist the students in the areas of comprehension, oral and written proficiency and cultural awareness.



School Library


City College Library is a resource centre for teaching, learning & research. It plays a proactive role in providing all kinds of academic resource contents and providing innovative, responsive and effective services to meet the changing needs of the students and Faculty. Library has E-Learning Center, Reference Section, Circulation Counter, Journals/Magazines and Newspaper Section are also available. The Library holds a hybrid collection of printed as well as electronic resources which include books, journals, databases, audio-visuals, CDs/DVDs, e-books, e-journals, reports, course materials; previous years question papers, Bound Volumes, Project Reports, case studies, etc. Currently the library holds over 14693 books, 48 print journals, provides access to Electronic Resources, newspapers and business magazines, and 12 multimedia resources. And also have Institution membership of NDL.
